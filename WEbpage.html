Project Overview: Kumbh Mela 2027, Nashik

An interactive analysis of the strategic deployment of an aerostat for persistent surveillance and crowd management at ritual sites. This proposal addresses key design, testing, and environmental challenges to ensure operational excellence and public safety.

Situational Context

Nashik is preparing for extremely high crowd density at defined bathing and ritual sites for the 2027 Kumbh Mela. Our aerostat solution provides persistent, wide-area surveillance, a critical capability for managing an event of this scale, especially under intense public and governmental scrutiny.
